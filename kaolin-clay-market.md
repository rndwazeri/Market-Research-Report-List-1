Calcined kaolin is subjected to high temperatures, enhancing its brightness and thermal properties, making it ideal for ceramics and plastics. Water-washed kaolin, through a purification process, offers high purity and is commonly used in paper and paint applications. Surface-modified kaolin undergoes chemical treatments to improve its performance in various applications, including cosmetics and rubber, catering to specific industry needs for enhanced functionality.</p></p>
