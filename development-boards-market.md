<p><p>Development boards play a crucial role in the electronics market, categorized into three main types. Evaluation boards are designed for engineers to assess the performance of semiconductor devices and systems, providing a platform to experiment with their functionalities. Demonstration boards showcase specific products or technologies, helping users understand features and capabilities through practical examples. Add-on boards enhance existing systems by providing additional functionalities or interfaces, allowing for customization and expansion in various applications. Together, they facilitate rapid prototyping and innovation.</p></p>
