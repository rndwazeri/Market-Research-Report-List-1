<p><p>The hemoperfusion apparatus market consists primarily of two main types: resin-based and activated carbon polysorbent filters. Resin hemoperfusion utilizes specific resins to effectively adsorb toxins and metabolites from the bloodstream, offering targeted treatment options for certain conditions. In contrast, activated carbon hemoperfusion uses porous carbon to adsorb a broader range of substances, making it versatile for various toxicological emergencies. Both types aim to enhance therapeutic efficacy and provide critical support in managing renal failure and drug overdose cases.</p></p>
