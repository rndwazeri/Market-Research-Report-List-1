<p><strong>The Dry Electrostatic Precipitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-Electrode</li><li>Moving-Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry electrostatic precipitator (ESP) market comprises two primary types: fixed-electrode and moving-electrode systems. Fixed-electrode precipitators feature stationary collection plates, with particles attracted to them when charged by high-voltage electrodes. In contrast, moving-electrode precipitators utilize electrodes that periodically shift, enhancing particle collection efficiency by minimizing re-entrainment. Both types are crucial for reducing airborne pollutants in industrial applications, with each design offering unique advantages in terms of maintenance, efficiency, and operational flexibility, catering to diverse environmental regulations and industrial needs.</p></p>
