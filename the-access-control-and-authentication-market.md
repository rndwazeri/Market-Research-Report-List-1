<p><p>The Access Control and Authentication market includes various types, primarily Discretionary Access Control (DAC) and Mandatory Access Control (MAC). DAC allows users to control access to their own resources, providing flexibility but potentially leading to security risks if not managed properly. In contrast, MAC enforces strict policies determined by a central authority, enhancing security by limiting user discretion. These access control models cater to different regulatory and operational needs, influencing their adoption across sectors like government, finance, and healthcare.</p></p>
