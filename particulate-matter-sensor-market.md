<p><p>The particulate matter sensor market is categorized into PM2.5, PM10, and other particulate types. PM10 sensors monitor particles up to 10 micrometers, which can affect respiratory systems. Other sensors may measure larger particles or specific types of pollutants, addressing diverse environmental monitoring needs across industries and regulatory frameworks.</p></p>
