<p><p>The thermoplastic geomembrane market consists of several types, including HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and others. HDPE is widely used for its durability and high chemical resistance, making it ideal for landfill and drainage applications. LLDPE offers enhanced flexibility and puncture resistance, suitable for various containment projects. PVC is appreciated for its cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Other materials include PP (Polypropylene) and EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), each tailored for specific environmental and application needs.</p></p>
