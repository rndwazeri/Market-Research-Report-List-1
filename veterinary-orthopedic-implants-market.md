<p><p>The Veterinary Orthopedic Implants market includes various types designed to support animal bone healing and joint stability. Plates are flat devices that stabilize bone fractures. Screws provide internal fixation by securing the alignment of fractured bones. Pins are used for transfixation of bone fragments, while wires support soft tissue and stabilize bones. Staples are utilized in wound closure and fixation. Joint replacement implants are crucial for severe joint diseases, enabling improved mobility and quality of life for animals.</p></p>
