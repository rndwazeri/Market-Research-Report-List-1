<p><ul><li>External Use</li><li>Internal Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pets Anti-Infective Medicine market is categorized into two primary types: external use and internal use. External use medications are applied topically to treat skin infections, wounds, or parasites, providing localized relief and protection. These often come in the form of ointments, creams, or sprays. Internal use medications, on the other hand, are administered orally or through injections to combat systemic infections, targeting internal health issues like bacterial or viral infections. Both types are essential for ensuring pets' overall health.</p></p>
