<p><p>Alkali resistant primers are essential for protecting surfaces from alkaline damage, commonly used in construction and renovation. The market features two primary types: pure acrylic and solvent mixture primers. Pure acrylic primers offer superior adhesion and durability, making them ideal for high-humidity environments. In contrast, solvent mixture primers contain a combination of solvents and resins, providing fast-drying properties and excellent resistance to moisture. Both types enhance surface integrity and prolong the lifespan of coatings applied over them.</p></p>
