<p><p>Amorphous Silicon (A-Si) Flat Panel Detectors (FPDs) are key components in medical imaging, categorized into indirect and direct conversion types. Indirect conversion FPDs utilize a scintillator to convert X-rays into light, which is then detected by A-Si photodiodes, allowing for improved image quality and sensitivity. Conversely, direct conversion FPDs directly convert X-rays into electrical signals, enhancing speed and efficiency. Both types are integral to advanced imaging systems, significantly impacting radiography and related diagnostic applications.</p></p>
