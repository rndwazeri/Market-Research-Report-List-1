<p><strong>The Full Dry Optical Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton</li><li>Stranded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Dry Optical Cable market encompasses two primary types: skeleton and stranded cables. Skeleton cables feature a compact, lightweight design with fibers positioned centrally, making them suitable for environments with limited space and where minimal weight is essential. In contrast, stranded cables consist of multiple optical fiber strands bundled together, providing enhanced flexibility and durability. This design is ideal for applications requiring greater bending capabilities and resilience against environmental stressors, making both types essential for various telecommunications and network infrastructures.</p></p>
