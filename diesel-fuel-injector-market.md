<p><p>The diesel fuel injector market primarily consists of two types: unit fuel injectors and dual fuel injectors. Unit fuel injectors combine fuel injection and fuel metering systems into a single unit, enhancing efficiency and space-saving in engines. They are commonly used in modern diesel engines for precise fuel delivery. On the other hand, dual fuel injectors facilitate the use of two types of fuel, allowing for flexibility in energy sources and improved performance in various applications. Both types are crucial for optimizing engine efficiency and reducing emissions.</p></p>
