<p><p>The silk screen photosensitive material market encompasses two primary types: photosensitive glue and photosensitive film. Photosensitive glue is used as an adhesive in various applications, particularly in printing and electronics, where its light-sensitive properties are crucial for creating precise designs. Photosensitive film, on the other hand, serves as a substrate for imaging and printing, allowing for high-resolution transfers on diverse surfaces. Both materials play vital roles in industries like textiles, electronics, and graphic arts, enhancing production efficiency and quality.</p></p>
