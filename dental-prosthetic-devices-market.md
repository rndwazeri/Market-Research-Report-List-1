<p><p>The dental prosthetic devices market comprises several key types, including bridges, crowns, dentures, and veneers. Bridges are used to replace missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth. Crowns cover damaged teeth for protection and restoration. Dentures are removable replacements for missing teeth, available as full or partial options. Veneers are thin shells that enhance the appearance of teeth by fixing imperfections. Other devices include implants and inlays, contributing to the diverse offerings within the dental prosthetics sector.</p></p>
