<p><p>The PET substrate siliconized film market comprises three primary types. Universal siliconized film is versatile and used in various applications, known for its low surface energy and release properties. Electrical insulating siliconized film is designed to provide electrical insulation in electronic components, offering superior dielectric strength. Laminating siliconized film is specifically tailored for use in laminating processes, enhancing adhesion and durability in layered products. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs, ensuring functionality and performance in diverse applications.</p></p>
