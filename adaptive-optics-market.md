<p><strong>The Adaptive Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavefront Sensor</li><li>Wavefront Modulator</li><li>Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adaptive Optics market comprises three main types: wavefront sensors, wavefront modulators, and control systems. Wavefront sensors detect distortions in incoming light caused by atmospheric turbulence or optical system imperfections. Wavefront modulators then adjust the light's phase or amplitude to correct these distortions, enhancing image quality. Control systems serve as the brain, processing data from sensors and directing modulators in real-time to achieve optimal correction. Together, they enable clearer imaging in applications like astronomy, ophthalmology, and laser communications.</p></p>
