<p><p>The Polyethylene Nucleating Agent market is segmented into two primary types: α Nucleating Agents and β Nucleating Agents. α Nucleating Agents promote the formation of small crystalline regions within polyethylene, enhancing its clarity, strength, and thermal properties. In contrast, β Nucleating Agents facilitate the development of a stable, finer crystal structure that improves impact resistance and transparency. Both types are essential for optimizing the performance and versatility of polyethylene in various applications, from packaging to automotive components.</p></p>
