<p><p>The residential HVAC equipment market includes two primary types: Direct Expansion (DX) Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. DX Systems utilize refrigerants to cool air and are typically installed in individual rooms or spaces, providing localized temperature control. Central Air Conditioning Systems, on the other hand, cool air at a central location and distribute it throughout the home via ducts. Both systems cater to different homeowner needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and comfort in climate control solutions.</p></p>
