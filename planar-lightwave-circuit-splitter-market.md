<p><strong>The Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1N</li><li>2N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) splitter market is categorized into 1N and 2N types based on their splitting capability. A 1N splitter divides the optical signal into N output ports, offering simplicity and efficiency for various applications. In contrast, a 2N splitter refers to a cascading arrangement where the splitting is achieved in two stages, resulting in a higher number of output ports (2N). This structure allows for more extensive distribution in telecommunication networks, enhancing flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
