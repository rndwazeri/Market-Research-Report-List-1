<p><p>The automotive leather interior market is primarily divided into two types: genuine leather and artificial leather. In contrast, artificial leather, or synthetic leather, is manufactured from polyurethane orPVC and provides a cost-effective, environmentally friendly alternative. It mimics the look and feel of genuine leather while being easier to maintain, making it popular in mid-range and budget-friendly vehicles.</p></p>
