<p><p>Peristaltic pumps, essential for mining and mineral extraction, facilitate efficient fluid transfer in harsh environments. Roller peristaltic pumps utilize rollers that compress a flexible tube, creating a consistent flow and minimizing shear stress on fluids, ideal for handling slurries. Shoe-type peristaltic pumps, on the other hand, feature a shoe that presses against the tube, allowing for gentle material handling with reduced maintenance. Both types ensure reliable operation in demanding conditions, enhancing productivity and operational efficiency in the mining sector.</p></p>
