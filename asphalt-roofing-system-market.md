<p><p>The asphalt roofing system market primarily comprises three types: Built-Up Roofing (BUR), Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), and Atactic Polypropylene (APP). BUR features multiple layers of asphalt and reinforcing fabrics, providing durability and waterproofing. SBS is a modified bitumen that offers enhanced flexibility and temperature resistance, ideal for diverse climates. APP, another modified bitumen, is known for its high-temperature tolerance and UV resistance. Each type caters to specific building needs, ensuring effective roofing solutions for various applications.</p></p>
