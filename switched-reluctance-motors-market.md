<p><p>Switched Reluctance Motors (SRMs) are a type of electric motor that operate on the principle of reluctance torque. They consist of a stator with concentrated windings and a rotor with a simple structure, typically made of laminated steel. SRMs are known for their robustness, high efficiency, and simplicity, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including electric vehicles, industrial automation, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The Switched Reluctance Motors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.41% during the forecast period.
