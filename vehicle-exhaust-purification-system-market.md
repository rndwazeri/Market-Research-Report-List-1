In contrast, the Oxidation Catalyst primarily targets carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons, converting them into less harmful substances through oxidation. Both systems play crucial roles in meeting stringent environmental regulations and improving air quality while enhancing vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
